Finally, Pennsylvania requires employers to carry workers’ compensation. Homeowners should verify coverage via the PA Workers’ Compensation Administration’s tools (often referenced as WCAIS) and ask for documentation showing the contractor’s WC policy or an exemption certificate.

Step-by-step verification for Tredyffrin homeowners: (1) Ask for the contractor’s IICRC credentials and current certifications; (2) Request a current certificate of insurance naming the homeowner’s address/interest (or at minimum the project’s insured party) and verify dates; (3) For pre-1978 homes, ask for the EPA Lead-Safe Certification and lead technician credentials; (4) Request workers’ comp proof/exemption paperwork; (5) Confirm whether your planned scope triggers permits—typically any electrical or plumbing repair/replacement, and any structural rebuild that requires township approval.
